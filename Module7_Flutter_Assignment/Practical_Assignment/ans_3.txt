Practical Que: 3
Ans:

1.Prepare Your App

 -Update the app version in pubspec.yaml.
 -Remove all debug print statements.
 -Set app icons using flutter_launcher_icons.

2.Build the Release AAB
-Run:
     flutter build appbundle --release
This generates the .aab file required by Google Play.

3.Create a Google Play Developer Account
  -Go to Google Play Console.
  -Pay the one-time $25 registration fee.

4.Create a New App in Play Console
  -Click Create App.
  -Enter app name, description, screenshots, and         icon.

5.Complete App Content Requirements
 -Add privacy policy.
 -Fill content rating form.
 -Choose target audience and permissions.
 -Select app category.

6.Upload the AAB File
-Go to Production → Create Release.
-Upload the generated AAB file.

7.Set Pricing & Distribution
-Choose if the app is free or paid.
-Select the countries where it will be available.

8.Submit for Review
-Click Review Release.
-Roll out your app to production.

9.Wait for Approval
-Google reviews the app (1–3 days).
-Once approved, your Flutter app becomes live on the Play Store.
